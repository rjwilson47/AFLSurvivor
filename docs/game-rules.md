AFL Survivor Pool — How It Works

The Basics

Each week you tip the loser of every AFL match on the card. You're predicting who gets beaten.

On top of your regular tips, you must nominate one match as your Main Tip for the round. Your Main Tip is the one that counts for your survival.

Lives

You start the season with 2 lives. Every time your Main Tip is wrong — or the match is a draw — you lose a life. Lose all your lives and you're eliminated. Last person standing wins.

The Main Tip Team Rule

You can only use each of the 18 AFL teams as your Main Tip once across the season. Six teams can be used twice. This means you can't just back Collingwood to lose every week — you have to spread your mains around and think ahead.

Idols

If you tip every single match correctly in a round (all your regular tips, not just the main), you earn an Idol. Idols are your safety net — play one before the second quarter of your Main Tip match and your life is protected that week, no matter the result. You can bank idols and use them whenever you need them most.

Playing an idol:
Must be used before the first bounce of the second quarter, via the app or by email to Mike.

Draws:
A drawn match is NOT a loss of a match, hence it IS a loss of life in the tipping game for both teams.

Deadlines

Regular tips close every Thursday at 5pm AEST. No exceptions. If you forget to submit, the home team of the last match is automatically assigned as your Main Tip for the round — so don't leave it to chance.

How to Log In

No passwords. Just go to the site, enter your email, and click the link that arrives in your inbox. Done. Bookmark the leaderboard so you can check in during the week.
